The Filling

1.

In a bowl combine melted butter, and coffee syrup. Whisk in the brown sugar, cornmeal and kosher salt.

2.

In another bowl crack and whisk the eggs, then add the cream, and vanilla and whisk until fully combined.
3.
Combine the two mixtures until just combined and pour into our blind baked pie crusts. Now they're ready to bake!
