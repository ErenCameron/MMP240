Baking and Eating!

1.

Preheat the oven to 350, and then place the blind baked shell on a parchment lined baking sheet and pour the filling into it until the it just reaches the edge of the pie.

2.

Put the sheet into the oven and bake for 45 minutes to an hour. or until the center jiggles only slightly when shaken. It will continue to set as it cools.
3.
Remove the pie from the sheet and cool for 4-6 hours. Once fully cooled and at room temperature, sprinkle generously with flaky sea salt, then slice, serve, and enjoy!
